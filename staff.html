Our Staff

What began as one woman’s passion for accounting and helping others navigate their finances has grown into a trusted, family-run tax and bookkeeping team.

Founded by Kim, our business was built on a simple belief: every client deserves accurate work, honest advice, and an experience where they never feel rushed. Over the years, that commitment to care and attention to detail became the foundation of everything we do.

As the business grew, it became a true family effort. Kim mentored and trained Raianna and Fiona through multiple tax seasons, passing on not only technical knowledge but also the values that matter most — integrity, patience, and genuine client care.

Today, we are proud to serve individuals and small businesses with a personal touch, whether in person or virtually. When you work with us, you’re not just another file — you’re part of a community built on trust, professionalism, and heart.

Kim

Kim holds a Business Degree with a major in Accounting and is certified in both personal income tax and small incorporated business taxes. With years of experience and a strong professional foundation, she is committed to providing accurate, reliable service to individuals and business owners alike.

As the founder and mentor behind the team, Kim prides herself on attention to detail and creating a welcoming, unrushed experience for every client. Her goal is simple: that each person leaves feeling confident, cared for, and completely satisfied with their service.

Raianna

Raianna trained under Kim for four tax seasons, gaining hands-on experience and building strong confidence in personal tax preparation. She holds certificates in Bookkeeping and Personal Tax Returns, allowing her to support clients with accuracy and professionalism.

She proudly welcomes clients in the Grande Prairie area into her home office, offering a comfortable and friendly environment. Raianna also provides virtual appointments, ensuring flexibility and convenience while maintaining the personal touch that defines the business.

Fiona

Fiona holds a Personal Tax Certificate and has completed three years of training under Kim's guidance. Through her dedication and attention to detail, she has become a valued part of the team.

Working both virtually and behind the scenes, Fiona helps ensure every return is entered accurately and carefully. She has a genuine thirst for knowledge and often spends her downtime reviewing CRA resources to stay current, so clients can feel confident their returns are handled with care.
